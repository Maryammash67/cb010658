SCUBA DIVIVNG

Boiler wrecks and a variety of reefs are two of Passikudah's most well-known features. Its deepest ends are the famous British Sergeant Wreck, which makes for fantastic underwater photos. Many are motivated by this wreck dive to comprehend how it came to be in Sri Lanka during World War 2. Visitors can see both large and little fish in this area because wrecks are being used as habitat by marine life. It wouldn't be surprising if you could become friends with the fish because they are accustomed to divers in this area. Visitors may take lessons at the local international diving school.

Location: Batticaloa, Eastern Province(Passikudah).

Price: Starts from LKR 17765 for newcomers as they are given training beforehand.
